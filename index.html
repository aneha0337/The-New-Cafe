About Us

"Cafe" is a restaurant that does not offer table service. Customers order their food from a counter and serve themselves. A cafe menu traditionally offers things such as coffee, espresso, pastries, and sandwiches.The goals of cafés are centered primarily around the customer. They aim to establish a balance between meeting the universal desires of patrons, such as comfort and privacy, and accounting for individual differences in user needs, such as independent workspaces.Our coffees are sourced from thousands of small coffee planters, who made us who we are today and we're glad to be a part of their lives. 'The New Cafe' to the youth is a “hangout” spot where they meet people, make conversations,and have a whole lot of fun over steaming cups of great coffee.It's been an exciting journey since then to becoming the largest organised retail cafe chain in the country.
